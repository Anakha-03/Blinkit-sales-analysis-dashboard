# 🛒 Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

The **Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard developed using **Microsoft Power BI**. The dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit's sales performance by outlet type, outlet size, outlet location, item category, and customer ratings. It enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, identify sales trends, and make data-driven decisions.

## Business Insights

- Total sales reached approximately **$1 million**.
- **Tier 3 outlets** generated the highest overall sales.
- **Medium-sized outlets** contributed the largest share of revenue.
- **Fruits & Vegetables** and **Snack Foods** were among the top-selling item categories.
- Supermarket formats significantly outperformed grocery stores in total sales.
- Sales peaked for outlets established around **2018**, indicating strong performance from relatively newer stores.
